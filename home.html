Pig - Dice Game Rules

Players

2

Goal

Be the first player to reach 100 points.

Gameplay

  • On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly until either:
  • A 1 is rolled. If a 1 is rolled, the active player's turn ends and no points are earned.
  • The player chooses to hold (stop rolling). If the player chooses to hold, all the points rolled during that turn are added to his/her total score.
